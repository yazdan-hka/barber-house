What is BraidStarz ?


Maybe you love Braid, maybe you don't. But BraidStarz, definitely does.

Like in the sky there are countless stars, it's the same on earth, but Braiders, waiting to shine.

There are a lot of Braiders around the world, which they should be known to public. Braid is an art, and a Braider is an artist.

What ever was the reason that these artists was not known as he/she should, now it is time to make some change.

BraidStarz is for those artists that they want to improve, grow and get better at their work.



Likewise, there are people all around the world, that they say to themselves:

"I want to braid my hair"

but they may run into a problem and say:

"I cannot find braider near me. Well, BraidStarz is here.

We have a braider finder page, which can be used by him/her who is looking for a braider in their location.



Generally, what we do is provide a place for braiders that can be a second home for them. Braiders who create an account on Braid Starz will have a profile. In their profile, all the necessary information can be displayed based on the braiders' decision. This includes a profile picture, location information, social media account IDs, name, and business name, all presented in a simple yet satisfying design. This allows customers who are looking for a braider in their area to find and connect with them.

Braiders have a dedicated space for uploading their works of art, showcasing their braid creations. Customers who visit their profile can check the quality of their braid work and establish a connection with them.

However, these are just the first steps of BraidStarz. We have many more features in incoming for braid lovers, which we are working on it.

All the uploaded media by braiders come together to create our collection of braids . Braid lovers can browse through our collection, scrolling until they find the perfect braid picture for them. By clicking on it, they can visit your profile if it belongs to you!
