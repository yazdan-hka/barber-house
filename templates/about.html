Who is BraidStarz ?


Maybe you love Braid, maybe you don't. But BraidStarz, definitely do.

Like in the sky there are countless stars, same on earth, but with Braids.

There are a lot of Braiders around the world, which they should be known to public. Braid is an art, and a Braider is an artist.

What ever was the reason that this artist was not known as he/she should, now it is time to make some change.

BraidStarz is for those artists that they want to improve, grow and get better at their work.

We provide useful services to them, so if they're hardworking they can make themselves known to people around them, and boost their business.



Likewise, there are people all around the world, that they say to themselves:

"I want to braid my hair"

but they may run into a problem and say

"I cannot find braider near me. Well, BraidStarz is here.

We have a braider finder page, which can be used by him/her who is looking for a braider near him, or in another city, or even country.

It is simple. you type your country or city, then the profile of those who did make an account in Braidstarz, will be listed for you and you can choose between them and see their profile. or you can see all of them!
